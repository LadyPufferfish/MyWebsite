Celeste

Imo the best game ever. It is a precision platformer so you WILL die a lot! But the controls are very fair, tight and fun. You can dash, jump and climb/jump away from walls. There is a very big modding and speedrunning community around this game, and everyone is genuinely super nice. Oh right and there are strawberries. Collect them. NOW!
